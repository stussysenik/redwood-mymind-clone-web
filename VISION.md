# BYOA Vision

**Build Your Own Algorithm** — a visual knowledge engine that feels native.

## Core Belief

The tools we use to save and organize inspiration should be as beautiful and tactile as the content itself. BYOA is not a bookmarking tool — it's a visual research platform where every interaction feels deliberate, every surface is alive, and the algorithm is yours.

## Design Pillars

### 1. Asset-First
Images, colors, and visual fidelity come first. Cards preserve original aspect ratios. Thumbnails load at every layer. No broken images — a 6-tier fallback chain ensures every card has a visual.

### 2. Native Feel
Haptic feedback on every interaction. Shake to shuffle. Swipe to navigate. 44px touch targets. The web app should feel indistinguishable from a native iOS app.

### 3. Connected Knowledge
Cards aren't isolated — they form a living graph through shared tags. The force-directed graph view reveals hidden patterns. The list view makes connections browsable.

### 4. Speed as a Feature
Viewport culling, Level of Detail rendering, Web Workers for physics simulation. The graph handles 1000+ nodes without jank. Every interaction responds in under 100ms.

### 5. Zero Noise
No error banners. No loading skeletons that flash. Silent re-extraction when images fail. The infrastructure is invisible — users only see their content.

## Target User

Creative professionals, visual researchers, and digital collectors who think in images and vibes. People who save 50+ things a week and need to rediscover them months later.

## North Star Metrics

- Time to first meaningful card: < 3 seconds
- Graph FPS at 500 nodes: > 30
- Image coverage (cards with visuals): > 95%
- Mobile Lighthouse Performance: > 90
