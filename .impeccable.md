## Design Context

### Users
Visual designers, researchers, and creatives who save content obsessively and care deeply about discovering unexpected connections between their ideas. Used as a personal knowledge archive — late night, often mobile, always in the middle of something creative. The interface competes with their creative instincts, so it must stay out of the way while still feeling alive.

### Brand Personality
**Crystalline · Deliberate · Connective**

BYOA (Build Your Own Algorithm). The Diamond icon is the north star — faceted, precise, refractive. Every surface should feel like it was cut, not poured. Speed is the primary sensory value: zero shadows, 2–3px radii, system fonts by default. Think Are.na crossed with a high-end art catalogue — institutional calm, editorial density, zero decoration for its own sake.

Anti-reference: anything that looks like a SaaS dashboard, a chrome extension popup, or a "modern productivity app."

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Dark-capable, light by default. The graph canvas is always dark (space). List/settings views follow system preference.
- **Color**: #0055FF primary accent. Tinted neutrals (blue-bias). OKLCH surfaces. No gradients on text. No glow.
- **Typography**: System font stack (as established by the BYOA `native` skin). Type hierarchy through weight and size contrast, not font families.
- **Motion**: Intentional only. State transitions (expand/collapse, select), not decoration. Grid-row easing, never height animation.
- **Density**: Information-dense but readable. This is a research tool, not a marketing page.

### Design Principles
1. **Tags are the graph's language** — shared tags are edges made visible. They should lead any connection display, not follow.
2. **Earn every pixel** — no decorative chrome, no side-stripe accents, no rounded-shadow cards without purpose.
3. **Mobile-first, touch-first** — 44px minimum touch targets, safe area insets, one-thumb reach for primary actions.
4. **Accessibility is non-negotiable** — WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Every interactive surface is keyboard-navigable and screen-reader labelled.
5. **i18n-ready from day one** — all visible strings flow through a messages object, never hardcoded.
